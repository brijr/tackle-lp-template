Busting Myths About Listing, Selling, and Managing a Cloud Marketplace Business

Register Now

What’s this Webinar about?

Cloud Marketplaces have a long history and as they have evolved into the revenue powerhouses they are today, many myths have made their way around the water cooler. As you’re looking at Marketplaces like AWS, Azure, and GCP, you may be asking or hearing questions like “Don’t we need to have a fully automated provisioning system? Isn’t Marketplace just for technical buyers? Do we just have to build it and they will come?” Let’s bust up these misperceptions about what it takes to get listed and start transacting on the Marketplaces.

What’s this Webinar about?

  1. What you need (and don’t need) to get started on Marketplace
  2. How to sell Marketplace to your customers (technical buyers and non-technical buyers alike)
  3. The realities of the cloud partnership and the positive impact of Marketplace
